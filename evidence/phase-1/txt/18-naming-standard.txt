# Naming Convention Standard

## Objective

Define a predictable naming standard for Terraform-managed resources.

## Pattern

`<project>-<environment>-<resource-type>-<suffix>`

## Example

- terraform-secure-foundation-dev-vpc-main
- terraform-secure-foundation-dev-subnet-public-a
- terraform-secure-foundation-prod-budget-monthly

## Rules

- use lowercase
- use hyphens as separators
- avoid spaces and underscores in final resource names
- keep names deterministic and readable
- environment must always be present
- abbreviate only when the provider imposes length constraints

## Derived Prefix

Primary prefix used by the environment root:

`<project>-<environment>`

Example:

- terraform-secure-foundation-dev
- terraform-secure-foundation-qa
- terraform-secure-foundation-prod
